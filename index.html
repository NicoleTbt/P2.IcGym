ABOUT US:

Hygiene is a priority in our facilities, the cleaning staff makes sure any time that all is 100% clean

All the facilities are modern and our energy system is one of the most environmental friendly in the region.

Guidelines

Main Lockers Gym Pool

Main

  1. Keep your ID card with you
  2. Respect the equipment in all facilities
  3. Food is not allowed inside the facilities
  4. No smoking indoors

Lockers

  1. Filming or taking pictures is not allowed
  2. Keep area clean
  3. Lock your belongings in the assigned locker
  4. Do not give your locker's key to others
  5. Return the locker's key before leaving

Gym

  1. Ask for help when needed
  2. Bring your own towel
  3. Put the weights back
  4. Avoid distracting others
  5. Filming is not allowed
  6. Avoid distracting others
  7. Respect the equipment
  8. Entrence for -16 is not allowed

Pool

  1. Have special slippers for the pool
  2. Do not enter with your outside shoes
  3. Do not swim with your clothes
  4. Cover your hair
  5. Do not run along side the pool
  6. Do not push others in the pool
  7. Dry off before going to other facilities
